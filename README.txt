This repo is freely available to anyone who is interested in and it consists of a collection of solutions to all problem sets exercises related to the course "Algorithm and Data Structures" (AY 23-24) held by Professor Polak in MSc A.I. (Bocconi University). To contribute to this repo you can follow instructions (see the file "instructions.txt" in the repo) to upload solutions that were assigned to you.
